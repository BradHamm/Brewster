Beer me bro! The best slogan for the best beer app on the internet, Brewster. Who are we? We are a community of beer drinkers dedicated to the craft, with the goal of documenting our passion to save our favorite brews, breweries, and all the in-betweens.

With this app, you are able to create a personal profile saving your favorite beers, ratings, reviews, and more! We also provide information on brewery vibes, location, and the like from well-established sources. Cheers to starting your Brewster journey!
